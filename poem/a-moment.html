A moment

On August 1st , 2021 by Vansika Pareek

I pursued the moment anyway,

I loved,

I laughed,

I kissed,

I cried,

I sinned the past, I cursed the future,

I surrendered,

not for the past, not for the future,

I surrendered for the moment,

because that moment was mine,

I pursued the moment anyway!
